19 October 2024

UiTM Perlis

About the Event

The Math & Net Mashup Day is a vibrant and exciting event organized by the College of Computing, Informatics, and Mathematics. It's a day full of fun, learning, and networking opportunities for students and faculty members alike. Come and join us for a memorable day filled with engaging activities and valuable experiences!

Objectives

  • To foster a sense of community among faculty students.
  • To promote healthy competition and teamwork.
  • To provide a platform for showcasing talents and skills.
  • To facilitate learning and personal growth through workshops and forums.

Activities

  • Explorace involving primary school students
  • Explorace involving UiTM students
  • Booth/food truck sales
  • Booth for iterative games by student association
  • Motivational forum from alumni
  • Python workshop
  • Canva workshop
  • Chess
